About Us

HoverSprite is a start-up Vietnamese agricultural solutions company based in the Southwest Provinces, specializes in organic fertilizer and pesticide spraying services for farmers, utilizing a fleet of 15 spraying drones to serve orchards, cereal fields, and vegetable farms.

Our Procedure

Currently, HoverSprite relies on a manual telephone-based system on the company's call center for farmers placing orders, where receptionists record the request and dispatch drone sprayers.
The sprayers then travel to the farm location, program the drones for spraying, and report completion via a video call chat app. Additionally, the receptionist gathers farmer feedback during the call.
