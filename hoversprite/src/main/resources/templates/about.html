About Us

HoverSprite is a start-up Vietnamese agricultural solutions company based in the Southwest Provinces. It specializes in organic fertilizer and pesticide spraying services for farmers, utilizing a fleet of 15 spraying drones to serve orchards, cereal fields, and vegetable farms.

Our Procedure

Services

HoverSprite used to run on manual telephone-based system for order processing. Currently, HoverSprite runs on live service system on website. Whenever there is new user, each user will be granted options to Create, Read, Update, and Delete orders under certain conditions such as each role has different tools.

Roles

Farmer
Farmer will only have tools to create within given procedure such as selecting specific date and review orders for further details.
Sprayer
Sprayer will be responsible for using drones to spray water at the farm on selected date under the guidance of order details.
Receptionist
Receptionist will update the order status as well as giving notifications about the status to both Farmer and Sprayer.

Feedback and Rating

Whenever an order is completed, Farmer role will send out feedback and rating to the appointed sprayer and HoverSprite company. From there, feedback and rating are updated and can be reviewed on live.
