Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about our services and drones.

HoverSprite specializes in organic pesticide and fertilizer spraying using advanced drones. Our services ensure optimal coverage, precision, and crop safety, with drones suited for different types of agriculture, including orchards, cereal fields, and vegetable farms.

You can easily book a spraying service by visiting our booking page. Once registered, you can select your crop type, choose the appropriate spraying service, and schedule a convenient time. You will also receive updates on your booking status.

Our drones adhere to the highest safety standards. We conduct regular maintenance and thorough inspections to ensure safe and reliable performance. Additionally, our operators are trained to follow strict safety protocols while spraying to minimize any risks to crops, animals, or people.
